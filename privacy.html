Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

Overview

Stunt Simulator is a free browser game site. We do not require an account, payment, or direct user profile to play. This policy explains what data is collected by the site, analytics providers, and embedded third-party game frames.

Information We Collect

  • Basic technical data such as page URL, referrer, browser, device type, approximate region, and event timestamps.
  • Gameplay page events such as page view, embedded player start, iframe loaded, direct play click, fallback click, fullscreen click, and guide views.
  • Contact information only if you choose to email us.

Analytics and Observability

We use Plausible self-hosted analytics, Google Analytics 4, and Microsoft Clarity to understand page performance, navigation issues, and player friction. These services may use cookies, local storage, session identifiers, aggregated reports, heatmaps, or replay-style diagnostics depending on your browser and provider settings.

Third-Party Game Iframes

Some games are embedded from third-party hosts such as unblocked-games.s3.amazonaws.com and games.crazygames.com. When you load or interact with an embedded game iframe, that third-party host may receive your IP address, browser data, and interaction data under its own policies. Direct Play links open those third-party game URLs in a new tab.

Advertising and External Sites

The current site code does not include a first-party ad purchase flow. If a third-party embedded game or external recommended site displays ads, those ads are controlled by that third-party service, not by Stunt Simulator.

Cookies and Storage

Analytics providers and embedded game providers may set cookies or local storage. You can block cookies or clear site data in your browser settings; some game or analytics functionality may be affected.

How We Use Data

  • Keep the game pages working and troubleshoot iframe/WebGL loading problems.
  • Measure which recovery actions players use, such as Direct Play, Restart, Fullscreen, and fallback links.
  • Improve SEO, performance, and mobile usability.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data may be processed by analytics providers, hosting/CDN infrastructure, and third-party game iframe providers as described above.

Your Choices

You can use browser privacy controls, ad/tracker blockers, cookie settings, and Do Not Track-style tools. You can also choose not to open third-party Direct Play links.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support(at)stunt-simulator.com.
